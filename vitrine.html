How to use

Auscultate your child at home

know the result right away

1

Turn on the stethoscope and the StethoBuddy application on your phone. The devices connect automatically.

2

Place the stethoscope on your child's chest. The system will guide you throughout the process, and will inform you if the measurement is wrong or the surroundings are too noisy

3

You can see if the examination shows the sounds characteristic of pneumonia and bronchitis or asthma exacerbations.
